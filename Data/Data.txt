To download our dataset from Kaggle, please follow these steps:

   1. Go to the Kaggle website and sign in to your account.
   2. Navigate to the “Datasets” section and search for the desired dataset 'Cats And Dogs'.
   3. Once you have found the dataset, click on the “Download” button to download it to your computer.

For your conveniece, you can download it directly from the following link:

		     https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/erodios/cats-and-dogs
